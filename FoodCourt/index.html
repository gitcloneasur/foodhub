FAMOUS ITEMS

MOMO

Momo is a type of dumpling originally from Tibet which is prominently found in the cuisines of Tibet and Nepal, as well as in the Indian states of Ladakh and Sikkim.

CHOWMEIN

Chow mein are Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu. chowmein is most famous in Nepal and india as a fast food.

THUPPA

Thukpa is a Tibetan noodle soup, which originated in the eastern part of Tibet. Amdo thukpa is a famous variant amongst the Indians, Tibetans and the people of Nepal.

SEKUWA

Sekuwa is meat roasted in a natural wood fire in a traditional Nepalese country style. While the meat is still raw, it is mixed with natural herbs and spices and other ingredients. Sekuwa can be made with pork, lamb, goat or chicken, or a mixture.

YOMARI

Yomari, also called yamari, is a delicacy of the Newar community in Nepal. It is a steamed dumpling that consists of an external covering of rice flour with sweet fillings such as chaku.

SEWALI

sewali is a food where meat is inside of dumpling and it is fried in oil.
